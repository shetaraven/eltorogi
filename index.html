Our Filipino Story

EL TOROGI began in 2020 in Ilagan City, with a passion for giving new life to quality pre-loved fashion. We source from local ukay-ukay markets across Luzon and carefully curate each piece to bring you affordable style with character.

Each item is hand-selected for quality, uniqueness, and durability. We wash, repair when needed, and style our finds to help you build a sustainable wardrobe that's both fashionable and kind to our planet.
